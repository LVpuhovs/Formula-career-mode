About Formula 1

Background

Formula 1, also known as F1, is the highest class of single-seater auto racing sanctioned by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). It has its roots in the early 20th century, with the first World Championship for Drivers established in 1950. Since then, Formula 1 has become one of the most popular and prestigious forms of motorsport, attracting millions of fans around the world.
